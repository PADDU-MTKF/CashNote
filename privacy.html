Privacy Policy

Cash Note does not collect, store or transmit personal user data.

The app is completely serverless and works offline. Financial data is stored only on the user's device.

Optional Google Drive backup allows users to store backup files in their own Google Drive account.

Permissions requested:

  • Notifications – for reminders and backup alerts
  • Google Calendar – optional reminder events
  • Storage – used only for local backup files
  • Google Account – used only for optional Google Drive backup

Users can disable these permissions anytime from the app settings.
