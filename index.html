Why Choose Cash Note?

Smart Expense Tracking

Record income and expenses quickly with a clean and modern interface. Manage money across wallets, bank accounts, or custom accounts.

Split Expenses Easily

Split bills with friends, roommates, or family and quickly track balances or debts directly within the app.

Reminder System

Never miss a payment. Set reminders for bills, debts, and custom events. Integrates optionally with Google Calendar.

Secure Backup

Protect your data with optional Google Drive backup or local on-device backup. Your data never leaves your device unless you choose so.

Privacy First

No servers. No ads. No tracking. No analytics. Cash Note works fully offline without needing an active internet connection.

Always Free

Cash Note is completely free to use and will remain free forever. No subscriptions, and no hidden costs.
