About Cash Note

Cash Note is built with privacy and simplicity in mind. Your financial data stays on your device. The app works fully offline and never sends your information to any server.

Features

Expense Tracking

Quickly record income and expenses with a clean and modern interface.

Split Expenses

Split bills with friends or family and keep track of balances easily.

Account Management

Track money across multiple accounts like wallet, bank, or custom accounts.

Reminder System

Never miss bills, payments or debts with built-in reminders.

Google Drive Backup

Optional cloud backup to your personal Google Drive for safety.

Privacy First

Cash Note is designed to respect your privacy.

The app is completely serverless. Your financial data is stored locally on your device. If cloud backup is enabled, encrypted backup files are stored only in your personal Google Drive account.

Permissions Explained

Notifications

Used for reminder alerts and optional auto backup notifications. Can be disabled anytime.

Google Calendar

Used only if calendar reminders are enabled to add or remove reminder events.

Storage Access

Used only to store local backup files in a dedicated app folder.

Google Account

Required only for optional Google Drive backup. The app can be used without signing in.
