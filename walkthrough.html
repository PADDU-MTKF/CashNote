Take a detailed look at the app's features through these guided screenshots. Learn how to make the most of your pocket manager.

Accounts & Debts

View all your cash locations at a glance. Manage multiple wallets, cards, and see exactly who owes you money or who you owe.

Quick Transactions

Adding an income or expense takes just seconds. Select your category, account, and enter the amount effortlessly with a clean UI.

Money Flow

Understand where your money is going. Visual charts and breakdowns help you analyze your spending habits over time.

Split the Bill

Easily divide expenses when eating out with friends or splitting rent. Track everyone's share and settle up seamlessly.

Smart Reminders

Never miss a payment again. Set custom reminders that can even sync to your Google Calendar automatically.

Settings & Security

Your data stays on your device. Configure offline backups or securely connect your Google Drive to keep your backups safe.
